Principles

Powerfully Pragmatic

Clever is pragmatic and builds solutions that solve our customers’ real problems today. You’ll find we love to set big, ambitious goals for ourselves to grow our business, but at the end of the day, we accomplish these goals not by building castles in the sky but with our feet firmly planted in the ground and our eyes and ears open to the complex and messy real world.

Predictable Clarity

At Clever, we emphasize clarity: at every step, it’s obvious what you can do and how things are going. This clarity is our main focus because of the complexity of what our product does and the seriousness of the consequences if someone makes a mistake. We should strive to make the product clear and straightforward to use, and never blame the user if a mistake is made.

Clever as the Co-Pilot

We sweat the details and understand the system so that our users have an informed and respectful guide to help them navigate complexity and ambiguity. Design experiences that provide a clear path forward but give our users the tools to step off the path on their own.

Consistently Great

Students show up to school every day. Teachers teach every day. So, like a school bus, Clever must show up for students and teachers every day. We must prioritize consistency, and introduce changes (especially during the school year) with care.

Typography

Typescale

The typescale determines the size of the text being used based off of REM, which is the root element modifier text, i.e. the base text size set by the browser.

Giant Text - 2.5 rem

Huge Text - 2 rem

Large Text - 1.5 rem

Regular Text - 1 rem

Small Text - .75 rem

Tiny Text - .625 rem

Type Weight

To limit type choices, Clever uses three font weights only.
