Principles

Knowledgeable Guide

We sweat the details and understand the system so that our users have an informed and respectful guide to help them navigate complexity and ambiguity. Design experiences that provide a clear path forward but give our users the tools to step off the path on their own.

Meaningful Clarity

At Clever, we emphasize clarity: at every step, itâ€™s obvious what you can do and how things are going. This clarity is our main focus because of the complexity of what our product does and the seriousness of the consequences if someone makes a mistake. We should strive to make the product clear and straightforward to use, and never blame the user if a mistake is made.

Meet Users Where They Are

At the risk of sounding obvious, Clever products focus on the actual problems experienced by educators and education technology developers. As we decide what features or products to build, we should be rigorous and thoughtful about grounding our decisions in the actual experiences of our customers.

Delightfully Powerful

Empower ours users to make them feel like they have superpowers in their jobs. Design experiences that put the best tools at hand before the users even reach for it and make light work of difficult tasks.
